WonderCode is not just an English x programming school.
We provide STREAM education that fosters 21st-century skills.

STREAM education is an educational model that began in the U.S. in the 2000s that combines STEM education (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) with Art and Robot/Reading elements.

Science 科学
Technology 技術
Robot / Reading ロボット / 読解力
Engineering 工学
Art 表現力 / 創造力 / リテラシー
Mathematics 数学

For example, in the WonderCode curriculum, students learn to use their creativity from stories, calculate angles and speeds, and learn engineering knowledge to make the robot run.

Students also learn the art of designing and building a workable program while observing movement and testing hypotheses to solve problems.

And the ability to develop sensitivity and express one's own opinions through theme studies and classes, and to create synergy through creative missions of teamwork are also necessary.

STREAM education is beginning to attract attention in Japan as an educational model that fosters the 21st century skills needed in the coming era.

・Information/ICT literacy 情報/ICTリテラシー
・Creativity and Innovation 創造力とイノベーション
・Learning how to learn 学び方の学習
・Critical thinking and problem solving skills クリティカルシンキングと問題解決力
・Communication and Collaboration コミュニケーションとコラボレーション

In addition to technical knowledge and logical thinking skills, WonderCord's curriculum fosters these 21st century skills to nurture future leaders who will be active in a global society.
